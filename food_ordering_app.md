## Low Level Design of the App

### Main Components
- **App component**: contains 3 main components
  - Header
  - Body
  - Footer

### Component Breakdown

#### Header
- App logo
- Navigation items
- Cart icon

#### Body
- Search Bar
- Restaurant Container
  - Restaurant cards
   - Image 
   - Name of Restaurant, Star Rating, Cuisine, delivery time

#### Footer
- Copyright disclaimer
- Links
- Address
- Contact information
